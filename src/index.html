About

MyWorkoutLog is a simple and easy way of logging your workouts and tracking your progress. Just think of it as your digital notebook!

Plan your workout

Add exercises to your log ahead of time so that you can stay focused on the task at hand.

Track your progress

Save your workouts and when you add an exercise to your log, Workout Log will tell you how you performed the last time you did that exercise.

See your growth!

MyWorkoutLog will save a short history of your workouts in the browser and give you statistics on your improvement.

MyWorkoutLog is designed to be used by experienced gym-goers, it will not teach you how to perform the exercises correctly.

How it works

MyWorkoutLog saves your workouts locally in the browser. No need to set up an account or log in, keeping it simple.

Instructions:

  1. Begin by clicking the Log Workout button at the top of the page.
  2. On the log screen, a workout page is displayed and you can alter the name of your workout and the date by clicking on these elements.
  3. Add exercises by selecting an exercise from the drop down menu and pressing the Add Exercise button
  4. You can also create your own exercise name by clicking on Create Exercise.
  5. Enter the weight and the reps for the exercise, then click the Add Set button.
  6. To delete a set, highlight the set and click the X icon that appears to the right of the set.
  7. To delete an exercise, hover over the exercise name and click the X icon that appears to the right of it.
  8. Once you have finished your workout, click the Complete Workout button on the bottom of the page to save it to the browser.
  9. Visit the History section to view past workouts and statistics on your improvement.
